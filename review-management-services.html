Our Approach

How we manage your reviews

Real-Time Monitoring

We track Google, Yelp, TripAdvisor, Facebook, DoorDash and more. You'll receive alerts whenever a new review appears.

Personalized Responses

Our hospitality-trained specialists respond to all positive and negative reviews with empathy and actionable solutions. We reference specific items on the menu and experiences to demonstrate attentiveness.

Fake Review Detection

If suspicious patterns appear — multiple one-star reviews from new accounts, references to items you don't serve — we document evidence and help you report them to the platform. We also advise on legal steps and extortion scams.

Insights & Feedback Loops

We can help you analyze reviews; this gives you great insights into what is really going on from a guest experience. You can get a clear picture of what is going well and what areas might have opportunities for improvement.

Review Request Campaigns

We can work with you to figure out how to get more 5-star reviews — be it using our review stands or personalized cards for every server!

Why It Matters

Why review management matters so much for restaurants

Negative reviews can cause stress, anger and even burnout among hospitality staff. It's even been shown that restaurants with an average under 4 stars see up to a 50% decline in business!

With Digital One Reviews, you gain peace of mind knowing that every guest is heard and that your brand is protected. We help you turn a potential crisis into an opportunity to shine.
