About Us

Passionate about restaurants and reputation

Digital One Reviews was founded by a marketing professional who saw firsthand how digital feedback could make or break a restaurant.

Our Mission

Giving restaurateurs back their peace of mind

We know that operators often feel at the mercy of review platforms and that keeping up with constant requests for feedback can feel overwhelming.

Our mission is to give restaurateurs back their peace of mind by turning reviews into tools for growth.

Our Values

What we stand for

Hospitality First

Every interaction — online or in person — should be handled with warmth and respect.

Transparency

We believe in honest feedback. We don't buy or fake reviews; instead, we encourage real guests to share genuine experiences.

Integrity

We never engage in pay-to-play or manipulate algorithms. We help you navigate opaque systems like Yelp's filter and fight back against review extortion.

Partnership

We become an extension of your team, collaborating with chefs, managers and owners to create a consistent brand voice across every channel.

Meet The Team

Hands-on since 2017

Digital One, a marketing agency, was launched in September 2017 with a focus on helping local businesses grow and stand out. With a strong understanding of the bar, restaurant, and hospitality industry, Digital One provides hands-on, results-driven marketing solutions tailored to each client.

The company specializes in reputation and review management, Google Business Profile optimization, and creative content, helping businesses strengthen their online presence, build customer trust, and turn feedback into opportunities for growth.
