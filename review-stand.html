The Digital Review Stand

A sleek countertop stand your customers simply tap with their phone — and land directly on your Google review page. No apps, no QR-hunting, no friction.

How It Works

Tap. Rate. Done.

The moment a customer tells you they loved their experience is the moment they're most likely to leave a review. The Digital Review Stand captures it — right at the counter, table or register.

Customer taps the stand

Built-in NFC works with any modern phone — a printed QR code covers everyone else.

Your review page opens instantly

No app download, no searching. Their phone lands directly on your Google review form.

The five stars roll in

Reviews arrive while the experience is fresh — and flow straight into your Digital One dashboard.

Why It Works

Frictionless asks get answered

Zero Friction

One tap replaces "find us on Google" — removing every step where a willing reviewer might give up.

Perfect Timing

Requests happen at the peak-happiness moment — checkout, after the compliment, before they leave.

Compounding Results

A steady drip of fresh reviews improves rating, ranking and the confidence of every future searcher.

Features

Small stand, serious hardware

  • NFC + QR combo — works with every smartphone, old or new.
  • Custom branded design — printed with your logo and colors to match your counter.
  • Direct-to-Google link — configurable to Google, Facebook or a smart landing page.
  • No batteries, no Wi-Fi — passive NFC means nothing to charge or maintain.
  • Dashboard tracking — see taps and resulting reviews in your monthly report.
