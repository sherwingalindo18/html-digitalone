Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Last updated: July 2, 2026

Digital One Reviews ("Digital One," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you visit our website at digitalonereviews.com (the "Site") or use our services. By using the Site or our services, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide

We collect information you give us directly — for example, when you fill out a contact or audit form, request a quote, subscribe to updates, or communicate with us. This may include your name, business name, email address, phone number, and any details you choose to share.

Information collected automatically

When you visit the Site, we may automatically collect certain technical information such as your IP address, browser type, device information, pages viewed, and the dates and times of your visits. We collect this information using cookies and similar technologies.

Information from third parties

When you engage us for services, you may authorize us to access information from third-party platforms (such as Google, Facebook, Yelp, or Apple) on your behalf to deliver those services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve our services and the Site;
  • Respond to your inquiries and requests, including audit requests;
  • Send you service-related communications and, where you have opted in, updates and offers;
  • Process transactions and manage your account;
  • Analyze usage to improve performance and user experience; and
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.

3. Text Messaging (SMS/MMS)

If you provide your mobile number and opt in, we may send you conversational and informational text messages related to your inquiry or account. Message frequency may vary, and message and data rates may apply. You can reply STOP at any time to opt out, or HELP for assistance.

No mobile information will be sold or shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent are not shared with any third parties, except for subcontractors or service providers (such as our messaging platform) strictly to help us deliver the messaging service.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate the Site, remember your preferences, and understand how the Site is used. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some features of the Site. We may also use analytics services that set their own cookies to help us measure traffic and usage.

5. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers who perform functions on our behalf (such as hosting, analytics, payment processing, and messaging), under obligations to protect your information;
  • Third-party platforms as needed to deliver the services you requested;
  • Legal and safety recipients when required by law, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Digital One, our clients, or others; and
  • Business transfers in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When information is no longer needed, we take reasonable steps to delete or de-identify it.

7. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information, or to object to or restrict certain processing. You can also opt out of marketing communications at any time by using the unsubscribe link in our emails or replying STOP to text messages. To make a request, contact us using the details below.

9. Children's Privacy

The Site and our services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

10. Third-Party Links

The Site may contain links to third-party websites and platforms. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties, and we encourage you to review their privacy policies.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Your continued use of the Site after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
